The Twirler

This is our most famous ride!

Take a moment to brace yourself and experience the feeling of flying. The Twirler will take you on an adventure that you will never forget. This suspended rollercoaster takes you in and out 3 full loops, multiple tight corners and 2 corkscrews. It is the newest addition to our park, and can be ridden by anyone over 140cm and under 130 kg.

Park information including accessibility, food and services

Food and drink

The park offers a range of different food venues; vegan and vegetarian options are availible. Free water fountains are scattered around the park. See map for details.

Vistors with small children


You can find family friendly bathrooms.

We are also equipped with atracctions for smallest children (3-6 years old).

Visitors with disabilities


The park has extra parking spaces for disabeled customers

Some rides might not be suitable for people with impaired movement. Inquire at the front desk for details.

How to find us

Directions description and opening times


To get to us from London:


  • Exit the city via A13
  • Change from A13 to A12 in Aveley in the direction of Ipswich
  • Take a right on A14 before Ipswich and follow the park signts

We are located a short ferry ride from the shore. Parking for the guests is availible next to the ferry and marked with the park banners.


Opening Times:

We are open every day from 10:00 to 18:00 except first Monday of the month, Christmas Day and New Year's Day.
