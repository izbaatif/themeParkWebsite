Tracey Hardin
This is the best theme park. The rides lived up to the hype, but I was most surprised by how delicious and affordable the food was. They even had vegan and vegetarian options. I recommend the nachos.
Hashir Rennie
Now that our kids are old enough for the rollercoasters, we bought the season pass. It was well worth the money to get to ride the Twirler as much as we want! We can't wait for the Whirlpool to open.
Scarlett-Rose Serrano
Being in a wheelchair, I was reluctant to visit the park but I was overwhelmed by how accommodating the park and staff were. There was preferred parking and ramps throughout. Even most of the rides were accessible. I had so much fun and will definitely be back!
